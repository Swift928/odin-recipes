Cheese Burger

Description

This is a picture of a very simple and traditional cheese burger. The cheese burger is famous and has been around for nearly a century. It was created by accident by Lionel Sternberger in the 1920's and has been with us ever since.

I'm going to show you how to create the iconic cheese burger with just a few ingredients. To make a simple cheeseburger, start by shaping ground beef into patties and seasoning with salt and pepper. Cook the patties on a preheated grill or stove-top pan over medium-high heat until they reach your desired doneness. During the last minute of cooking, top each patty with a slice of cheese and cover to melt. Toast the burger buns, assemble the burgers with lettuce, tomato, pickles, onion, and condiments of your choice, and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Onion
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties on a preheated grill or stove-top pan over medium-high heat until desired doneness.
  3. During the last minute of cooking, top each patty with a slice of cheese and cover to melt.
  4. Toast the burger buns.
  5. Assemble the burgers with lettuce, tomato, pickles, onion, and condiments of your choice.
  6. Serve and enjoy!

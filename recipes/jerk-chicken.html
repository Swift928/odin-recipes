Jerk Chicken

Description

Jerk chicken is a traditional Jamaican dish known for its bold and spicy flavor. The chicken is marinated with a mixture of fiery spices and herbs, then grilled or roasted to perfection, resulting in a deliciously tender and aromatic dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (whole or parts)
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Green onions (scallions)
  • Thyme (fresh or dried)
  • Garlic cloves
  • Ginger (freshly grated)
  • Ground allspice
  • Ground black pepper
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Ground nutmeg
  • Brown sugar
  • Soy sauce
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Lime juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the jerk marinade by blending together scotch bonnet peppers, green onions, thyme, garlic, ginger, allspice, black pepper, cinnamon, nutmeg, brown sugar, soy sauce, apple cider vinegar, lime juice, olive oil, and salt.
  2. Cut the chicken into pieces or use whole chicken and make deep incisions in the meat to allow the marinade to penetrate.
  3. Coat the chicken with the jerk marinade, ensuring it covers each piece evenly. For the best flavor, marinate the chicken for at least 2 hours or preferably overnight in the refrigerator.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat or use an oven set to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Grill or roast the jerk chicken for about 25-35 minutes, turning occasionally until the chicken is cooked through and has a slightly charred appearance.
  6. Remove the chicken from the grill or oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  7. Serve the delicious jerk chicken with your favorite side dishes and enjoy!

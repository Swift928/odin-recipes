Shaggy Dog Roll

Description

The Shaggy Dog sushi roll is a delectable and popular sushi creation. It typically features tempura-fried shrimp, creamy avocado, and cucumber, all rolled in sushi rice and seaweed, and then topped with a generous layer of sweet and tangy eel sauce and a sprinkle of crunchy sesame seeds.

Ingredients

  • Sheets of nori (seaweed)
  • Sushi rice (prepared with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt)
  • Tempura-fried shrimp
  • Avocado (sliced)
  • Cucumber (julienned)
  • Eel sauce (unagi sauce)
  • Tempura flakes (tenkasu)
  • Soy sauce (for dipping, optional)
  • Pickled ginger (for serving, optional)
  • Wasabi (for serving, optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare sushi rice following your preferred method and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. Place a sheet of nori (seaweed) shiny side down on a bamboo sushi rolling mat.
  3. With wet hands, spread a thin layer of sushi rice evenly over the nori, leaving about half an inch of nori at the top edge uncovered.
  4. Arrange tempura-fried shrimp, avocado slices, and cucumber sticks horizontally along the center of the rice.
  5. Using the bamboo mat, carefully roll the sushi away from you, tucking in the fillings tightly as you go.
  6. Moisten the uncovered nori edge with water to seal the roll.
  7. Once the roll is fully formed, sprinkle tempura flakes on top and press them gently into the rice to adhere.
  8. Cut the roll into bite-sized pieces using a sharp, wet knife.
  9. Drizzle the top of the sushi pieces generously with eel sauce.
  10. Serve the Shaggy Dog sushi roll with pickled ginger, wasabi, and soy sauce for dipping, and enjoy!
